Cookies & Tracking

Cookie information for visitors and advertising transparency.

This page explains how cookies, analytics tools, and advertising technologies may be used on the BHBD TRAINING LTD website.

Cookie Policy

How cookies and similar technologies may be used

This website may use cookies and similar technologies to support essential functionality, improve performance, understand visitor behaviour, and measure advertising effectiveness.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. Some cookies are necessary for site operation, while others help with analytics, security, and advertising measurement.

Types of cookies we may use

  • Essential cookies to support basic page functionality, navigation, or security.
  • Analytics cookies to understand page visits, traffic sources, and website engagement.
  • Advertising cookies to measure Google Ads performance, conversion activity, and remarketing where enabled.

Google advertising technologies

Where Google Ads or related services are used, Google may set or read cookies and identifiers to help measure ad interactions, report conversions, limit repeated ad display, and improve campaign relevance.

Control & Consent

How users can manage cookie preferences

Managing cookies

  • You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings.
  • You may also manage ad personalisation preferences through your Google account settings where available.
  • If a cookie banner or consent tool is present on this website, you can use it to adjust your preferences for non-essential cookies.

Consent

Where required by applicable law, non-essential cookies should only be used after obtaining valid user consent. Essential cookies may still operate because they are necessary for the website to function properly.

Third-party services

Third-party providers such as Google may process cookie-related data according to their own privacy terms and settings. We encourage users to review those terms directly where relevant.

Contact

If you have questions about this Cookie Policy, contact appointments@bhbdtraining.com.
